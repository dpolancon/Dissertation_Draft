# S2.2: Profitability Analysis

## Notes

## Key Decomposition
Four-channel identity:
`r̂ = μ̂_t + p̂_t + B̂r_t + π̂_t`

where:
- `μ`: capacity utilization
- `p` (Py/PK): relative price of output to capital
- `Br`: real capital productivity at normal utilization  
- `π`: profit share

Standard Weisskopf (1979) three-channel misattributes price dynamics to technology channel.

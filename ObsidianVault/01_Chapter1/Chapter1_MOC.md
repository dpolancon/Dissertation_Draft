# Essay 1: Critical Replication of Capacity Utilization

> *Overaccumulation and Capacity Utilization Measurement: A Critical Replication of Anwar Shaikh's Cointegration Method*

## Central Argument

This essay critically replicates [[Shaikh2016]]'s cointegration-based identification of [[ObsidianVault/91_Concepts/capacity_utilization]] as a structural benchmark. The replication proceeds in four stages (S0–S3), testing whether Shaikh's ARDL/VECM identification strategy is robust to specification geometry, data choices, and temporal composition. Key finding: the identification holds under S0–S2 but temporal instability emerges in S3, with implications for the theoretical claim of a gravitational long-run [[ObsidianVault/91_Concepts/capacity_utilization]] norm.

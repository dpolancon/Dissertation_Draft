# Equation — ARDL Error-Correction Mechanism

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch1:eq:ardl_ecm`
**Form:** ECM reparameterization of the ARDL levels equation.

Reparameterizes the levels ARDL into first differences + lagged levels (the Pesaran–Shin–Smith bounds-test form). The long-run levels terms appear as λ_y·y_{t-1} + λ_k·k_{t-1} + λ_e·e_{t-1}, with the bounds test applied to their joint significance (F-test and t-test on λ_y).

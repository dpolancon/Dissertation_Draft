# Equation — Balance-of-Payments Identity

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch3:eq:bop_identity`
**Form:** `Δ(e_t × IR_t) = CA_t + KA_t`

Where:
- `CA_t` — current account balance
- `KA_t` — capital account balance (net capital inflows)
- `Δ(e_t × IR_t)` — change in reserve holdings at domestic-currency value

## Economic interpretation

The BoP identity: the change in the domestic-currency value of reserves equals the sum of current and capital account flows. Under capital controls (UP period), KA is restricted, making CA the primary lever for reserve accumulation.

## Notes

This identity is the bridge between the external constraint (CA+KA) and the internal leverage ratio dynamics (LevR). See [[eq_levr_lom]] for the full law of motion.

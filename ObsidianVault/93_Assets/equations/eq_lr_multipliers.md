# Equation — Long-Run Multiplier Recovery

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch1:eq:lr_multipliers`
**Form:** `b̂ = −ψ̂_k(1)/φ̂(1)` and `θ̂ = −ψ̂_e(1)/φ̂(1)`

Recovers the long-run multipliers b (capital elasticity) and θ (transformation elasticity) from the ARDL lag polynomial sums. The identification target θ̂ is the ratio of the sum of e-lags coefficients to the sum of y-lag coefficients (with sign reversal).

# Equation — ARDL Levels Specification

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch1:eq:ardl_levels`
**Form:** `φ(L)·y_t = ψ_k(L)·k_t + ψ_e(L)·e_t + c₀ + c₁·t + Σδ_h·D_h + ε_t`

Where:
- `φ(L)` — lag polynomial on y (p lags: φ₁...φ_p)
- `ψ_k(L), ψ_e(L)` — lag polynomials on k and e (q lags each)
- `c₀, c₁` — intercept and trend
- `D_h` — dummy variables (level shifts, structural breaks)
- `δ_h` — dummy coefficients

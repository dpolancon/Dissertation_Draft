# Table B2 — Four-Channel Decomposition of Profit-Rate Swings

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch2:tab:B2_peaktotrough`
**Caption:** "Four-channel decomposition of profit-rate swings, US NFC, 1951–1974."
**File:** `Chapter2/tables/tab_B2_peaktotrough_US.tex`

## What it shows

Peak-to-trough decomposition of the nominal profit rate r_t for the US Non-Financial Corporate sector over the Fordist era. Each swing is decomposed into the four Weisskopf channels: μ̂ (capacity utilization), p̂ (relative price), B̂r (real capital productivity), π̂ (profit share).

## Interpretation notes

The decomposition is the empirical core of the Basu (2019) crisis-channel identification. Dominant channel in the profit-rate decline identifies the operative crisis mechanism. See [[crisis_theory]] and [[profit_rate]] for theoretical context.

# Table — Basu (2019) Typology Grid

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch2:tab:basu_grid`
**Caption:** "Basu typology grid."

## What it shows

Basu (2019) three-channel crisis taxonomy: profit squeeze (falling π̂), demand deficit (falling μ̂), realization crisis (falling B̂r / p̂). Maps each channel to its empirical signature in the Weisskopf decomposition.

## Structure

| Channel | Mechanism | Empirical signature |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| Profit squeeze | Rising wage share erodes margins | Falling π̂ dominant |
| Demand deficit | Insufficient consumption or investment | Falling μ̂ dominant |
| Realization crisis | Overaccumulation / falling productivity | Falling B̂r or p̂ dominant |

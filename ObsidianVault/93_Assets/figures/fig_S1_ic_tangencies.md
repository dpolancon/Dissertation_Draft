# Figure S1 — IC Tangency Points (ARDL Fit–Complexity Plane)

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch1:fig:S1_ic_tangencies`
**Caption:** "IC tangency points in the ARDL fit–complexity plane."
**File:** `Chapter1/figures/fig_S1_ic_tangencies.pdf`

## What it shows

Tangency points between the information criterion frontier and the admissible specification envelope in the (fit, complexity) plane. Each point is a local IC optimum; the global optimum is the BIC/AIC winner within the admissible set.

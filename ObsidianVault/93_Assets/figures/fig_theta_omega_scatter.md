# Figure — MPF Schedule: Transformation Elasticity vs Wage Share

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch2:fig:theta_omega_scatter`
**Caption:** "MPF schedule: transformation elasticity vs wage share."
**File:** `Chapter2/figures/fig_theta_omega_scatter.pdf`

## What it shows

Scatter of θ̂(ω) estimated values against the wage share ω, tracing the mechanization possibility frontier. Points on the MPF correspond to cost-minimizing techniques; the slope gives the structural identification of θ.

## Interpretation notes

The downward slope (θ < 1 over the Fordist range) indicates over-accumulation: capital deepening outruns output growth. The knife-edge ω_H where θ = 1 marks the Harrodian boundary.

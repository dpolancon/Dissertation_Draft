# Figure B4 — Nominal Profit Rate with Identified Turning Points

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch2:fig:B4_profit_rate`
**Caption:** "Nominal profit rate with identified turning points, US NFC Sector, 1940–1978."
**File:** `Chapter2/figures/fig_B4_profit_rate_turning_points.pdf`

## What it shows

The nominal profit rate r_t for the US Non-Financial Corporate sector from 1940 to 1978, with turning points identified by the Stage B decomposition. The turning points demarcate peak-to-trough intervals used in the four-channel decomposition (Table B2).

## Interpretation notes

The figure anchors the Fordist profit-rate trajectory: the long postwar boom peaking ~1966, followed by the Great Stagflation decline. The four-channel decomposition in [[tab_B2_peaktotrough]] decomposes each swing into μ̂, p̂, B̂r, and π̂ contributions.

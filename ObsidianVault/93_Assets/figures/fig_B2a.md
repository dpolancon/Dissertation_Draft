# Figure B2a — Cumulative Four-Channel Contributions to Profit-Rate Growth

**LaTeX label (canonical):** `ch2:fig:B2a`
**Caption:** "Cumulative four-channel contributions to profit-rate growth."
**File:** `Chapter2/figures/fig_B2a.pdf`

## What it shows

Stacked area chart of cumulative contributions from the four Weisskopf channels (μ̂, p̂, B̂r, π̂) to aggregate profit-rate growth over the Fordist era. Visualizes which channel drove the long boom and which led the decline.

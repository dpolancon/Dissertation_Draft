# Mechanization Possibility Frontier (MPF)

## Definition
The long-run locus of technically efficient (capital intensity, capital productivity) combinations available to the economy at a given state of technique. Formally: a cointegrating relationship between `ln(Br)` and `ln(k)` with slope `θ` (the transformation elasticity).

## Role
The MPF is the structural identification device for capacity utilization in the dissertation:
1. Cointegration of `[ln(Br), ln(k)]` identifies θ
2. The Error Correction Term (ECT) from the MPF VECM recovers the structural capacity utilization series
3. CU = deviation of actual `Br/k^θ` from its long-run frontier level

## Peripheral Extension
For Chile, the MPF is augmented with a BoP cointegrating vector (propensity to import) and a threshold VECM for the BoP regime switch. See [[ch2_S2_5_Peripheral_Extension]].

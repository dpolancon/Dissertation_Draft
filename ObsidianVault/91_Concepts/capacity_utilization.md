# Capacity Utilization (μ)

## Definition
The ratio of actual output to potential output at normal (full) utilization of productive capacities. Distinct from the *desired* utilization rate.

## Theoretical Tensions
- **Survey-based measures** (McGraw-Hill, Federal Reserve G.17): based on managers' self-reported capacity
- **Structural/econometric identification** (Shaikh): recovered from the cointegrating relationship between capital intensity and capital productivity — the Mechanization Possibility Frontier
- **The controversy**: whether there exists a stable long-run *normal* utilization rate that governs accumulation dynamics, or whether utilization is endogenous to demand

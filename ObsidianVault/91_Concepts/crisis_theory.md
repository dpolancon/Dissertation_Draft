# Marxist Crisis Theory

## Basu (2019) Taxonomy
Three structural channels through which the profit rate can contract:

| Channel                  | Mechanism                                          | Indicator       |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Profit squeeze           | Distribution: rising wages erode π                 | π̂ < 0 dominant |
| Demand deficit (Okishio) | CU falls: capacities built but demand insufficient | μ̂ < 0 dominant |
| Realization crisis       | Relative prices (Py/PK) compress                   | p̂ < 0 dominant |

"The four-channel decomposition adds the **technology channel** (Br̂) as a separate factor." 
**False Claim**

## Structural Crisis vs. Normal Cycle
- **Normal cycle**: single-channel dominance (one mechanism at a time) → partial institutional reconfiguration sufficient
- **Structural crisis**: multi-channel simultaneous drag → institutional settlement cannot contain

Empirical finding: US 1966–70 — π (34.5%), μ (29.8%), Br (29.2%) all drag simultaneously. No offsetting channel. Structural crisis of Fordism.

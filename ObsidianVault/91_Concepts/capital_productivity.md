# Capital Productivity

## Two Concepts
- **Nominal capital productivity**: `Y/K` in current prices — includes both price and quantity effects
- **Real capital productivity at normal utilization (Br)**: `Yn/K` in constant prices — pure technology channel

## The Three-Channel vs. Four-Channel Split
Weisskopf's three-channel framework uses nominal `Y/K`, conflating:
`(Y/K) = (Py/PK) · Br`

The four-channel decomposition separates these, revealing that the 1972–74 US contraction was *price-mediated* (oil shock), not a technology failure.

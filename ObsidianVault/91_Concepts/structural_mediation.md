# Structural Mediation

## Definition
The way in which macro-level accumulation dynamics are mediated by structural conditions (productive capacities, institutional configurations, technological paradigm) — distinct from purely demand-driven or supply-driven accounts.

## Role in Essay 1
Developed in [[ch1_S2_2_Structural_Mediation]] as the conceptual bridge between:
- The reproduction schemes (Marx Vol II) — disproportionality as structural condition
- The empirical identification via VECM — which recovers the structural attractor

# Profit Share (π)

## Definition
Share of gross value added accruing to profits (net of wages and intermediate inputs):
`π = Profits / GVA`

## Role in Profitability Decomposition
Distribution channel in the four-channel profit rate identity.
A declining π signals a *profit squeeze* — distributional pressure from rising wages relative to productivity.

## Fordist Pattern (US)
- Modest compression from 37% (Early Fordist) to 36% (Late Fordist)
- Compression driven by demand management politics committed to full employment
- 1966–70: profit share fell 34.5% of the compounded profit rate decline — institutional ceiling of full-employment policy

# Dependency Theory

## Schools (Ch3 Taxonomy)
| School | Key figures | Central claim |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| Classical dependency | Frank, Cardoso-Faletto, Marini, Bambirra | Core-periphery surplus extraction; structural underdevelopment |
| Chicago Boys / neoliberalism | DeCastro, Valdes | Market failure → reform; Allende's collapse = policy failure |

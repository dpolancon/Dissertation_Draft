# Synthesis Checklist

## Before writing a synthesis note
- Have I specified the crisis mechanism?
- Have I specified the Chilean conjuncture?
- Have I specified the mediations?
- Have I said whether this is cyclical or structural?
- Have I linked the note to at least one concept and one case?

## Before writing a chapter section
- Do I know what each source is doing?
- Am I mixing concept definition with historical evidence?
- Am I clear about what is specifically Chilean?
- Am I avoiding single-cause explanation?
- Have I linked national dynamics to the world market where needed?

# Post-1990 Neoliberal Order in Chile

## Problem
How did neoliberalism become durable after dictatorship?

## Working hypotheses
- institutional locking-in,
- export-led growth with social fragmentation,
- household indebtedness,
- pension-finance articulation,
- selective incorporation through consumption and targeted policy.

## Key question
Was this a stable accumulation regime, or a fragile political settlement masking deeper contradictions?

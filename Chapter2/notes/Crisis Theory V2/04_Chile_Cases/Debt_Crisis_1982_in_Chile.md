# Debt Crisis 1982 in Chile

## Why it matters
This is a decisive moment for showing that neoliberal restructuring did not eliminate crisis. It generated new forms of vulnerability.

## Analytical angles
- finance-led instability,
- external debt dependence,
- exchange-rate and balance-of-payments pressures,
- state rescue of private accumulation.

## Questions
- How did debt postpone contradictions?
- What failed in the initial neoliberal design?
- How did the crisis reshape the later neoliberal order?

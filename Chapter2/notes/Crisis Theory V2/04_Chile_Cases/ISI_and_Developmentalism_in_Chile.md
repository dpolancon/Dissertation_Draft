# ISI and Developmentalism in Chile

## Central problem
Import-substitution industrialization attempts to reduce dependence and expand domestic industrial capacity, but often produces new disproportionalities and external bottlenecks.

## Questions
- Did ISI stabilize accumulation or merely displace contradictions?
- Which sectors were strengthened?
- How did external dependence re-enter through technology, inputs, or finance?

# Dictatorship Neoliberal Restructuring 1973-1982

## Core idea
The dictatorship is not just political repression followed by policy change. It can be interpreted as a forced restructuring of class relations, accumulation, and institutional form.

## Questions
- Was neoliberalism here a response to crisis, a political project, or both?
- What was destroyed in order to create new profitability conditions?
- How were finance, labor discipline, and state form reorganized?

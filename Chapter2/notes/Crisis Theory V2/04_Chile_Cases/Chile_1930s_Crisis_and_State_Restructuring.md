# Chile 1930s Crisis and State Restructuring

## Why this matters
This is a key hinge for thinking the collapse of an externally dependent export pattern and the expansion of state-led developmental responses.

## Analytical angles
- crisis of export dependence,
- external constraint,
- institutional reorganization,
- opening toward new accumulation strategies.

## Questions
- Was the rupture mainly external or already internally overdetermined?
- What kind of state restructuring followed?
- How did the crisis reconfigure class alliances?

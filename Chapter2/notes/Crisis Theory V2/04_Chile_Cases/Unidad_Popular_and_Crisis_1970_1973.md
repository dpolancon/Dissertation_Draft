# Unidad Popular and Crisis 1970-1973

## Why this matters
This conjuncture can be read simultaneously as:
- a crisis of accumulation,
- a crisis of class power,
- a crisis of state form,
- and a crisis of international and domestic stabilization mechanisms.

## Productive question
What exactly is “the crisis” here?
- inflationary breakdown?
- distributional struggle?
- political sabotage?
- exhaustion of a prior regime?
- attempt at transition blocked by capitalist power?

# Crisis Theory to Chile Bridge

## Purpose
This note translates abstract crisis theory into a Chile-focused research workflow.

## Step 1: Identify the theoretical mechanism
Examples:
- profitability squeeze,
- realization blockage,
- disproportionality,
- external bottleneck,
- finance-led fragility,
- crisis of regulation.

## Step 2: Identify the historical conjuncture
Examples:
- 1930s,
- ISI bottlenecks,
- 1970-1973,
- 1982,
- 2019.

## Step 3: Identify mediation
Never jump from abstract theory directly to event. Insert mediations:
- class conflict,
- state form,
- international dependence,
- institutional architecture,
- sectoral structure.

## Step 4: Decide crisis type
- cyclical,
- structural,
- political,
- legitimacy,
- social reproduction,
- combined.

## Output
A good Chile interpretation should generate:
- a mechanism,
- a conjuncture,
- a mediation chain,
- and a claim about the kind of crisis involved.

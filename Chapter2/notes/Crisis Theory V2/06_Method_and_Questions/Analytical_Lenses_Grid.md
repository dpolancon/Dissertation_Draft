# Analytical Lenses Grid

| Lens | Main mechanism | Best Chile use | Main blind spot |
|---|---|---|---|
| Marxian profitability | Profit squeeze / overaccumulation | restructuring, devaluation, class conflict | may underplay external constraint if isolated |
| Realization / demand | sale and demand blockage | domestic market weakness, demand fragility | may underplay production structure |
| Disproportionality | sectoral mismatch | ISI bottlenecks, tradable constraints | can become descriptive without theory of power |
| Regulation approach | regime and institutional stabilization | dictatorship, post-1990 order, 2019 crisis | needs clear mechanism to avoid vagueness |
| Dependency | external subordination | export dependence, debt, foreign exchange | can flatten internal class dynamics if overstretched |
| Financialization | debt and finance-led fragility | 1982, pensions, household debt, post-1990 Chile | risks reading everything through finance |

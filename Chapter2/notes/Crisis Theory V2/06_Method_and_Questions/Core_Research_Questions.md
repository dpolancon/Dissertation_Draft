# Core Research Questions

## Main question
How can the theory of crisis be used to interpret Chilean historical development without collapsing political, economic, and international determinations into a single-cause explanation?

## Secondary questions
1. Which crisis theories travel best to the Chilean case?
2. Which moments in Chile are cyclical crises and which are regime crises?
3. How should external dependence modify standard crisis theory?
4. How do state restructuring and class conflict mediate crisis outcomes?
5. When does neoliberalism appear as stabilization, and when as deferred crisis?

## Comparison prompt
For each theoretical family ask:
- What is the primary contradiction?
- What is the crisis mechanism?
- What is the empirical signature?
- What does it explain well in Chile?
- What does it leave underexplained?

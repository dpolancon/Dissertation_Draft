# Profitability

## Why it matters
Many crisis theories treat the movement of profitability as central to the rhythm of accumulation.

## Analytical uses
- as a signal of investment incentives,
- as a synthetic expression of distributive and technical conditions,
- as a trigger for restructuring.

## Internal questions
- Is profitability falling because wages rise?
- because productivity weakens?
- because capital intensity rises?
- because realization conditions deteriorate?
- because external conditions tighten?

## Chile angle
Profitability analysis in Chile often cannot be separated from:
- commodity dependence,
- exchange-rate pressures,
- labor repression or discipline,
- and international finance.

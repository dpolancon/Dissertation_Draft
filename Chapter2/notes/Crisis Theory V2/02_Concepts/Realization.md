# Realization

## Definition
Realization concerns the conversion of produced surplus into monetary form through sale.

## Why it matters
A system may produce surplus but fail to realize it smoothly. This pushes crisis theory toward demand, circulation, money, credit, and world-market relations.

## Analytical question
Is the blockage located in production, distribution, circulation, final demand, or external markets?

## Chile angle
In Chile, realization questions often intersect with:
- export dependence,
- domestic market weakness,
- income concentration,
- credit-led expansion,
- and external shocks.

# Crisis

## Working definition
Crisis is not just a downturn. It is a **moment of rupture or intensification of contradictions** in which the existing form of accumulation loses coherence, legitimacy, or reproducibility.

## Possible dimensions
- economic crisis,
- political crisis,
- crisis of social reproduction,
- crisis of legitimacy,
- crisis of international insertion,
- ecological crisis.

## Key distinction
A crisis can be:
1. **cyclical** — a disturbance internal to the normal movement of accumulation, or
2. **structural / regime-level** — a breakdown of the institutional and social conditions that previously stabilized accumulation.

- external bottleneck crises,

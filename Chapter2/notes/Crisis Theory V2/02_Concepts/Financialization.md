# Financialization

## Working idea
Financialization refers to the growing weight of financial logics, actors, assets, and constraints in the reproduction of the economy and of everyday life.

## Crisis relevance
Finance can:
- defer crisis,
- displace contradictions,
- intensify fragility,
- and reorganize class power.

## Chile angle
In Chile this can be linked to:
- pension funds,
- household indebtedness,
- conglomerate power,
- international integration,
- and the post-dictatorship reproduction of neoliberalism.

# Capacity Utilization

## Definition
Capacity utilization measures the relation between actual output and some benchmark of productive capacity.

## Why it matters for crisis theory
It can indicate:
- unused productive potential,
- profitability pressure,
- demand weakness,
- or the instability of an accumulation regime.

## Caution
The concept is theoretically loaded. Its meaning depends on how “capacity” is defined and measured.

## Chile angle
Useful for connecting:
- macroeconomic instability,
- industrial structure,
- underutilized productive resources,
- and the politics of policy coordination.

## What this pack is for

The pack is designed to answer a question like:

> How should the theory of crisis be mobilized to interpret the Chilean historical trajectory without reducing crisis either to pure economics or to pure politics?

The structure encourages movement between:
- abstract theory,
- mid-level mechanisms,
- historically specific Chilean conjunctures,
- and source governance.

# Post-Keynesian and Kaleckian Crisis Approaches

## Core idea
Demand, investment, distribution, and expectations play a central role in macroeconomic instability.

## Useful themes
- effective demand,
- capacity utilization,
- distribution-led vs profit-led patterns,
- debt and financial fragility,
- policy coordination failures.

## Why useful for Chile
Helpful when the analytic focus is on:
- domestic demand constraints,
- wage repression,
- investment weakness,
- and instability mediated by finance.

## Caution
Some versions may be too closed-economy or too aggregate unless linked to:
- external constraint,
- uneven development,
- and class-state restructuring.

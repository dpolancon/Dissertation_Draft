# Dependency and Uneven Development

## Core idea
Peripheral and semi-peripheral formations cannot be read as delayed copies of the center. Their crises are shaped by dependent insertion into the world market.

## Why useful for Chile
Chile’s trajectory raises recurring issues of:
- export specialization,
- foreign-exchange dependence,
- technological dependence,
- external debt,
- and vulnerability to world-market shifts.

## Use
This school is especially useful when crisis seems tied to:
- external bottlenecks,
- commodity cycles,
- subordinate financial integration,
- and class structures shaped by external articulation.

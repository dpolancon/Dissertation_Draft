# Marxian Theories of Crisis

## Core concern
How can capitalist accumulation generate the conditions of its own disruption?

## Main lines often emphasized
- profitability and the tendency of the rate of profit,
- overaccumulation,
- realization problems,
- disproportionality,
- devaluation and restructuring.

## Use for Chile
Strong when the question concerns:
- accumulation dynamics,
- class conflict,
- restructuring,
- and crisis as a violent reorganization of social relations.

## Tension to watch
Avoid reducing every Chilean rupture to one single mechanism. Marxian frameworks are often strongest when used with mediations:
- world market,
- state form,
- finance,
- dependency,
- institutional change.

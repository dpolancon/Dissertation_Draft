# Regulation Approach

## Core idea
Capitalism persists not because contradictions disappear, but because historically specific institutional forms temporarily organize and stabilize them.

## Key pairs
- accumulation regime,
- mode of regulation.

## Why useful for Chile
It helps interpret crisis as:
- breakdown of a social-institutional configuration,
- not just a recession,
- not just a policy mistake,
- not just a class conspiracy.

## Good questions
- What held the regime together?
- What contradictions were displaced?
- What new institutional fix emerged after crisis?
